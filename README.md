# 🏥 Healthcare Survey Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Healthcare Survey Dashboard** is an interactive Excel-based analytics dashboard designed to analyze healthcare data for hospitals and insurance companies. It provides insights into patient distribution, medical conditions, insurance billing, medications, and admission trends.

This dashboard enables healthcare administrators and insurance providers to make **data-driven decisions** through clear and interactive visualizations.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze healthcare survey data across hospitals and insurance companies  
- Monitor total number of patients and revenue  
- Identify frequently occurring medical conditions  
- Compare insurance billing patterns  
- Analyze admission trends  
- Track medication usage  
- Provide actionable business insights

## 🧠 Key Insights

- Arthritis, Diabetes, and Cancer show the highest number of patients.  
- United Healthcare has the highest billing amounts among insurance providers.  
- Asthma patients typically require longer hospital stays.  
- Certain medications such as Lipitor and Ibuprofen are widely used.  
- Billing costs vary significantly across different medical conditions.
